Find the support you need, when you need it most.

Hotlines

National Mental Health Hotline

Phone: 866-903-3787

Availability: Free, confidential, and available 24/7.

This hotline offers support for individuals experiencing anxiety, stress, and other mental health concerns. Speak with trained professionals for guidance and resources.

988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline

Phone: 988

Availability: Free, confidential, and available 24/7 across the U.S.

If you are experiencing mental health-related distress or are worried about a loved one, this lifeline provides immediate crisis intervention, emotional support, and connections to local resources.
